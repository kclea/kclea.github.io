Princess Rosie Cotton

Rosie is a female long-haired calico cat with golden eyes. She was gifted to me for my seventh birthday. Rosie was very feisty for her first ten years of life but has started to mellow out. She loves sunshine, being outside, food, and attention (on her terms). 

Nala

Nala is a neighborhood cat that adopted my family when we moved to South Carolina. He is a brownish gray color with dark strips and aqua eyes. Nala is extremely friendly and relaxed. He loves attention and boxes.

Butterscotch

Butterscotch is another neighborhood cat that semi-adopted us when my family moved to South Carolina. He is a light orange long-haired cat with yellow eyes. He is very timid and will rarely let anyone get near him let alone touch him. Butterscotch loves food and hiding from people.
